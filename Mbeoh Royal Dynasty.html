Mbeoh Royal Dynasty

Mbeoh Palace

Mbeoh is a village situated to the North East of Lebang,Fontem Subdivision, Lebialem Division of Cameroon and about 13 km from Menji, housing the Subdivisional and Lebialem Divisional Headquarters. The founders of this chiefdom came from Atoh(Ataw) in Dschang led by Kwewung towards the end of the 18th century.

After a brief stay at Atel- Andeng kwewung then moved into the forest zone with his followers and founded his chiefdom along the upper valley of Ntse- Mbeoh (river). He put up his palace at Afouh in Lewoh Fondom today.

It is to be noted that Mbeoh is among the autonomous villages of the German map of December 1922 by Gadman. after the Germans Fontem Asonganyi started making moves for Fombeoh Atabonglefac to come to him.

Mbeoh Village has a Development Association code-named "MBECUDA" created in 1988 by Hrh Fombeoh Ndemalia, 7th

His Royal Highness Fombeoh Ndemalia

He succeeded his father - Fombeoh Atabonglefac in 1964 and has since ruled Mbeoh till present day.

Fombeoh Atabonglefac made his exit on 13th December 1964 and Fombeoh Ndemalia took over the Mbeoh thrown after a long period of Regency of Mbe Keah Linus who was later on replayed for a short period by Pa Johnny Esehmoh.

He was brought to the Fon of Fontem by the Fon of Foto Dschang who raised him. He was installed the same day - 18th April 1976.The fon of Fontem thanked the fon of Foto in his speech then presented the “new Fombeoh” to the public amidst thunderous applauses. He was let to his seat with Mafua and Nkweta by the late chiefs’friends, Bekem Mbeoh and family members. This was followed by jubilation and merry making. It was a great day for all the Mbeoh who came from far and near for that Historic Day. (Asaha had died at his very young age).
