Executed and Ongoing Projects

  • MBECUCA mobilised resources and financially supported the construction of the Lebang road
  • MBECUCA through her benefactors and donors, contributed to the cleaning of the Mbeoh palace, hospital and hall. This has been left unattended due to the ongoing crisis in the North West and South West Regions of Cameroon
  • MBECUCA keeps providing aid andassistance to the internally displaced inhabitants of the Mbeoh community and the English Speaking Cameroonians in general
  • MBECUCA is currently working and mobilizing resources to support the solar energy project of the Lebang community

    • UPCOMING PROGRAMMS FOR KIDS,WOMEN, YOUTHS AND SENIORS

    MBECUCA has as its heart of project to help the following groups of people

    Kids

  • we shall be teaching the “nweh “language to the kids so that they will be able to express themselves in the Nweh Language.
  • Mbeoh traditional meals and story telling about Mbeoh will be taught to kids and goodwill individuals to preserve Mbeoh's cultural and traditional heritage

    • Women

  • we shall be training and assisting women by teaching them how to cook traditional meals
  • we shall be training and teaching women dancing and singing according to the nweh culture
    • .

    Youths

  • We shall be training and assisting the youths by improving their skills on traditional meals.
  • We Shall be teaching them how to dance the nweh traditional dance.
  • Educational and training programs shall be made available to empower youths with knowledge and skills necessary for personal growth and development.

    • Seniors

  • We will be providing seniors with resources that will help them to hand down the Nweh norms and values to their generation through story telling, historical facts, arts and craft.
