About

Mbeoh Cultural and Development Association Canada International (MBECUDA) is committed to empowering the sons and daughters of MBEOH and its environs.

MBECUDA was created in 1988 by Hrh Fombeoh Ndemalia, the 7th and registered on 24/03/2011. Ref No 95/37/D14/1/vol1/BAPP of 24/03/2011.

It's pioneer was Nkemtem Johnson,followed by Nkemasong Keah Vincent, Nkemkweh-mbeoh Ambrose and Dr Nkematechong David. The current President is Nkemtanyi John.

MBECUDA exists to serve as a bridge between the Mbeoh community in Canada and the broader society, facilitating their well-being, cultural preservation, socio-economic growth, and political engagement. The organization also aims to be a force for positive change in Cameroon, supporting development projects and providing aid to those facing the challenges of conflict and instability.
