MBECUDA exists to serve as a bridge between the Mbeoh community in Canada and the broader society, facilitating their well-being, cultural preservation, socio-economic growth, and political engagement. The organization also aims to be a force for positive change in Cameroon, supporting development projects and providing aid to those facing the challenges of conflict and instability.
